App description

You are police officer, who has investigated the murder of his friend without finding out anything. You don't realize how the murderer did it literally without leaving any trace or clue. Your only clue is the last conversation with your friend, where he looked exhausted, tired, and his eyes lacked the spark of life that had been there before. When you asked was he feeling well, he just said, "There are demons in the world who manage to escape eternal damnation to avenge the life that imprisoned them. And those it targets cannot escape it" and then he left without explanation. You wondered about it, but because of your hard work, it didn't resurface in your mind until he was brutally murdered. The murder shocked all the townspeople, and some left the city out of fear. You and the other police officers are trying to bring the murderer to justice, but due to a lack of any clues, you haven't progressed. As before, you go to bed hoping that the next day you will find the murderer, and tonight your wish will come true, but not necessarily in the way you want. It's time to face the same demon your friend had: but will you be able to escape the trap of your own memories, or will you suffer the same fate as your friend?

This is horror game created by AttackFilm1790.
